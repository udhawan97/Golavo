# Workspace notes

## Golavo website

- The public website is the Astro/Starlight project in `docs-site/`.
- The landing page is a product-led marketing surface; the remaining routes are
  documentation and should keep Starlight's information architecture.
- The primary conversion is **Download or run locally**. Methodology and source
  exploration are secondary paths.
- The established visual language is Japanese editorial: sumi ink, washi paper,
  gold-leaf data accents, and a single vermilion hanko commitment accent.
- Product trust is part of the brand: deterministic models own every forecast
  number; optional AI can explain an evidence bundle but cannot alter a forecast.
- Real product screenshots, the match-programme GIF, and the existing explanatory
  SVGs are the preferred media. Decorative stock imagery is out of scope.
