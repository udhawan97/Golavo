## 90 minutes of evidence

Golavo is the football app whose headline feature is that **it can be checked**. It seals a forecast before kickoff — with its model version, feature snapshot, and the content hashes of every source it used — and scores it after full time. No revisionism. No "AI predictions." Just numbers that keep a receipt.

<CardGrid stagger>
  <Card title="The engine owns the numbers" icon="approve-check">
    A deterministic Dixon-Coles / bivariate-Poisson model produces every probability. AI never edits a number — it cites, explains, and researches. [Methodology →](/Golavo/methodology/prediction/)
  </Card>
  <Card title="Honest about data" icon="warning">
    Open-core data is results-grade and redistributable. Depth (lineups, corners, xG) is bring-your-own-key and stays on your machine. [Coverage →](/Golavo/data/coverage/)
  </Card>
  <Card title="Local-first & private" icon="laptop">
    No account, no telemetry, no ads. Keys live in your OS keychain. The only default network call is the update check — and you can turn it off. [Privacy →](/Golavo/privacy-security/)
  </Card>
  <Card title="Not a betting product" icon="close">
    No odds, no picks, no "locks," no bankroll advice, no affiliate links. Ever. [Principles →](/Golavo/introduction/)
  </Card>
</CardGrid>
