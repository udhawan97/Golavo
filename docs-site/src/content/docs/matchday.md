Golavo is organized around the matchday. (These surfaces arrive in Phase 2.)

## Matchday

Your favorites first: today's and upcoming fixtures for the teams and countries you follow, each with a forecast summary and freshness stamp.

## Fixture Room

Everything for one match, in tabs:

- **Forecast Theatre** — the sealed forecast: a W/D/L probability bar with an uncertainty band, the exact-score matrix (with an honest tail bucket), and — where data allows — likely scorers and expected corners. Missing markets are shown as missing, not guessed.
- **Commentator's Notebook** — cited, source-backed facts and milestones, with coincidences clearly quarantined ("for the pub, not the forecast").
- **AI Deep Read** — optional narrative and scenario analysis, always visually subordinate to the sealed numbers (Phase 6).
- **After the Whistle** — once full time arrives, the sealed forecast is scored against the actual result, with its contribution to running calibration.

## Team, player & manager dossiers

Factual profiles built from CC0 data and Wikidata — form, records, and history — with original artwork, never protected imagery.

## Data & Model Health

Source freshness, the discrepancy queue, pack versions, and calibration status — so you can always see how current and how trustworthy the underlying data is.
